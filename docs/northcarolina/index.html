North Carolina voting statistics

Manually updated:
Have patience, please do not refresh!!! North Carolina updates its data once-daily and does not push out continuously like Florida SOS does.

This map is meant to explore demographics, not serve as a 100% accurate count. Here's why:
The map is compiled from the entire NC voter history records, which is not always consistent.

How this map was implemented. It took me a long time just to figure out how to encode the entire 5+gig NC voter history on the client - so it'll be a little slow.
Alter checkboxes to enable filtering over demographics. Multiple checkboxes within a category act as inclusive operators.

NOTE: Not every category adds up to 100%! (E.g., many voters do not give out demographic data.) This is why "all categories checked" will look different than "none checked."
